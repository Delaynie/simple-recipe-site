Ingredients

  • 4 Tbls of Creamy or Crunchy Peanut butter
  • 2 Tbls of Grape Jelly (any flavor/brand of jelly will do as long as it's not KY)
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Optional: 4 Doritos Nacho Cheese Chips (for Garnish!)

Instructions

  1. Add pre-measured peanut butter onto one slice of bread (with a knife or fingers, idgaf)
  2. Add pre-measured jelly to the other plain slice of bread
  3. Place pieces of bread together (pb and j facing each other) and then eat.
