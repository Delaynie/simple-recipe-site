Ingredients and Tools Needed:

  • One package of bacon (maple is superior, but make do with what you have, I guess)
  • Cookie Sheet
  • Non stick aluminum foil
  • Tongs
  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Line cookie sheet with the foil (don't be stingy, let it be enough to curl up on the sides to keep the grease contained)
  3. Transfer slices of bacon onto lined cookie sheet
  4. Put bacon into the oven and set timer for *10-12 minutes
  5. Once bacon is slightly browning, pull out of the oven and use the tongs to flip the bacon to their other side
  6. Set timer for additional *5 minutes or until bacon is cooked to your liking

*Cooking times may vary
